FAQ

What’s included?

A personalized habit map: daily timeline, hydration targets, movement snacks, snack matrix, sleep anchors, coach notes, and a grocery quick list—delivered as a PDF and mobile‑friendly view.

How long does it take?

We don’t promise delivery times while the engine is manual. If timing is critical, include that in your email—we’ll reply quickly with feasibility.

Do you offer refunds?

If we haven’t started building your plan, you can cancel. Once delivered, refunds are case‑by‑case. Our goal is to make your plan useful—reach out and we’ll make it right.

Will my plan update over time?

Yes—especially with a coach. Quick‑Start plans can be refreshed later. Coach‑Linked plans evolve as your habits do.

How do you handle privacy?

We design local‑first. Your data stays yours. We use the minimum necessary information to build your plan—no ads, no resale.

Can coaches use this with clients?

Yes. Email us to discuss Coach’s Clipboard access and Coach‑Linked DITL maps.
