What I Bring

The Methodology

What started as personal survival became systematic innovation. The Evolutions framework transforms behavioral change from wishful thinking into engineering methodology—iterative two-week sprints that build resilience through documented process rather than raw willpower.

My Drexel doctoral research focuses on longitudinal adherence through trust dynamics, exploring why some people stick with goals beyond arbitrary deadlines while others abandon them. This isn't theoretical work—it's field study validation of the system I built to engineer my way back from death.

The patent-pending AI coaching platform represents the technical embodiment of this methodology: systematic behavioral intelligence that amplifies human expertise rather than replacing it. Filed February 2025, it's infrastructure for scaling transformation, not automating relationships.
